Smoked Gouda Mac & Cheese with Jalapeño Crunch

This ain’t your kid’s boxed mac. Creamy smoked gouda, crispy jalapeño breadcrumbs, and enough butter to make Paula Deen blush.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded smoked gouda
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 diced jalapeño

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta al dente. Drain.
  2. Melt butter, stir in milk, add cheese until melted.
  3. Stir pasta into sauce. Top with panko + jalapeño, broil until golden.
