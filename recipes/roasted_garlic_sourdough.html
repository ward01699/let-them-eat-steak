Roasted Garlic Sourdough Loaf

Rustic. Chewy. Garlicky. This sourdough hits all the right notes whether you’re slathering on butter or building a next-level grilled cheese.

Ingredients

  • 500g bread flour
  • 350g water
  • 100g sourdough starter
  • 10g salt
  • 1 bulb roasted garlic

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, water, starter. Rest 30 min. Add salt + garlic. Fold over 4 hours.
  2. Shape. Proof. Bake in Dutch oven at 475°F for 20 min covered, 25 min uncovered.
