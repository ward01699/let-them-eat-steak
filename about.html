Hi, I’m Lena — welcome to my backyard kitchen!

I grew up in a big Cuban-American family where everything revolved around food — birthdays, arguments, reconciliations… all handled at the grill or over the stove. My abuela taught me to marinate steak like it was holy ritual, and my dad insisted that charcoal was the only way to cook anything worth remembering.

After years of balancing a marketing career by day and grilling with a glass of wine by night, I decided to build Let Them Eat Steak as a home for all my favorite recipes — the indulgent, the rustic, the nostalgic, and the wildly new.

Whether I’m perfecting a butter-seared ribeye or baking sourdough at sunrise, I’m here to share recipes that are bold, flavorful, and totally doable in your own kitchen.

When I’m not cooking, you’ll find me barefoot by the pool, planning the next dinner party I’ll inevitably overdo — and loving every minute of it.

Thanks for stopping by. I hope you leave hungry.

– Lena
